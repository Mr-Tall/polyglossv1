\l This is a grave that Kaʕbo son of Ḥāreθat constructed for Raqōś daughter of ʕAbd-Manōto, his mother, and she perished in al-Ḥegro year one hundred and sixty two in the month of Tammūz. May the Lord of the World curse anyone who desecrates this grave and anyone who would open it, with the exception of his children, and may he curse anyone who would bury or remove from it (a body).
